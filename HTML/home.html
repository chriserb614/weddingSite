Chris + Eryn

June 22, 2019 • Orange, CA

Our Story


We met the same way most millennial couples meet these days- on the internet. After a week of shy texts and wink emojis, we agreed to go on our first date which also happened to be on John and Sarah’s 39th wedding anniversary.


If you ask Chris, he will tell you that her smile lit up the restaurant.


If you ask Eryn, she will tell you he made her laugh the whole evening!


Flash forward two weeks to a walk in the park where we held hands and talked about our lives. We said our “I love you’s” on that date in that park and two years later, in the same park, Chris got down on one knee and proposed.


We have been so blessed to have each other through loss of loved ones, loss of jobs, changes in careers and homes, birthdays, babies, laughter and tears and now we get to call each other husband and wife.


We cannot wait to share in this joyous celebration with you all!
